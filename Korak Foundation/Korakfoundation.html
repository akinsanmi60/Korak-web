Welcome to KoRak Foundation

KoRak Foundation is a non-governmental organization (NGO) set up as an outreach focused on maternal and child health, adolescent sexual health, nutrition, education, and deworming, tackling child slavery and exploitation, increasing opportunities for girls and young women. Also, men and women suffering domestic violence in abusive relationships.
